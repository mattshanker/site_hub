Web Development

"How does the same website look great on big AND small screens?"

Responsive Web Design is the technique of using media queries, adaptive content and grid layout to allow your site to respond to fit any screen. Things like adding a schedule, color picker or menu to your site or maybe adding a date picker to your contact form so people can suggest their own appointment times are things I can do to help make your site more useful to first-time, repeat, and potential customers.

The important thing is to not let the tool of responsive design become the goal. The real goal is to build a website that is both fast and useful, for everyone.

As of April 2015, Google's search algorithm has already started taking into account websites that aren't optimized for mobile, so it's more important than ever to design with mobile in mind. [TechCrunch]
