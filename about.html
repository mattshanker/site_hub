I don't work in...

Back-end development: server-side development has it's own set of quirks that demand a developer's full attention. I focus on the front-end, also referred to as "client-side". You can't avoid the server entirely (you have to deploy your site somewhere) but PHP forms and deployment are easier to handle for me than a database.

App development: iOS apps are written in either Objective-C or Swift, and Android apps are coded in Java, which has zero in common with JavaScript. I'm focused on the browser.

Design starting from scratch: Do yourself a favor and hire a designer if you're starting from a blank page. They can give you the best idea of what's possible with line and color. I do that too, but for your business on the web.
