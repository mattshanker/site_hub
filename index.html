Responsive Web Design

Media queries, adaptive content and grid layout allow your site to respond to fit any client's screen. Now customers don't have have to pinch and zoom your desktop site. Get the feel of an app without the app store.

Dynamic vs Static

Content Management Systems aren't the right choice for everyone and the added complexity can make them a headache for anyone not making use of the CMS a few times a month. Static sites are built and served using only HTML/CSS/JS and content updates to these sites come from making changes to these files directly. If you don't need to change you content more than 1 or 2 times a month and would rather have a more secure and maintainable site, ask about making your project static.

Landing pages - your site's welcome mat
