About Me

Originally from NJ, I started digging into websites in my teens to find out how they worked, and what was wrong when they didn't. I enjoyed it, but didn't see it as a serious career option.

Go Terps

I decided to go to school for marketing and when I left, I felt marketers were considered commodities, and therefore replaceable. I didn't want that and decided to learn to code.

Self-taught coder

I started by trying to make a phone app but couldn't get it work. Then I began to learn HTML, CSS3 and JS and have been coding for over a year. I also learned to work with Node/NPM/Gulp and Git during that time.

Automation

Marketing automation gives your customers an alternative to a phone call to get more information about your business. It means guiding quick buyers, browsers, and return customers through your site to deliver the information they need, when they need it. Things like adding a schedule, color picker, menu, a date picker help make your site more useful to these customer groups.

Onboarding

Creating an on-boarding process for your business can give you back your time to focus on delivering the best product or service for your customers. Onboarding gives your customers a better experience by being ready to assist them any time. As the owner, you can spend your time with qualified customers who've already requested additional information about your business.
